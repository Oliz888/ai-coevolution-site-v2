v1 → v2.1 Expansion

The original corpus (110 papers) was expanded with 139 papers from OpenAlex systematic search and backward citation analysis, adding new characters and works not found in v1.

Interpretation: The corpus shows mixed saturation signals. New characters continue to appear (ratio 1.58), driven by the long tail of peripheral figures in new papers. However, works are approaching saturation (ratio 0.09) — the canonical sci-fi works are well-covered. The core anchor characters are stable — the top 20 have not changed since v1. The growing long tail is expected for an interdisciplinary corpus spanning STS, humanities, and HRI.

Backward Citation Coverage

As a complementary validation, we extracted reference lists from 82 corpus papers (4,504 individual references) and cross-matched them against the full 249-paper corpus. This tests whether the papers our corpus cites are themselves included — a standard bibliometric completeness check.

78.1% of corpus papers (193 / 247) appear in at least one other corpus paper's reference list, indicating strong internal cohesion. Only 8 genuine academic gaps were found at the core topic level (sci-fi + robots/AI + society), and none are foundational works. The top 2 gaps (Sartori & Bocca 2023, Cave et al. 2018) have now been added to the corpus.

v1 → v2.1 Expansion

The original v1 corpus (110 papers) was expanded with 105 papers from an OpenAlex systematic search. The expansion added 51 entirely new characters and 23 entirely new works not found in the v1 corpus, confirming that the additional source broadened coverage meaningfully.

Interpretation: The corpus is not yet saturated. The last 20 papers contribute new characters at 1.9× the rate of the first 20, and new works at 1.0×. Q3 and Q4 together add 377 new characters and 202 new works. Additional sources (Scopus, Google Scholar, expert recommendations) would likely continue to discover new material. The search is thorough but not exhaustive — expected for an interdisciplinary corpus spanning SF literature, robotics, and cultural studies.
